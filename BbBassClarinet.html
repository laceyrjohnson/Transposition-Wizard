Transposing for the

Bb Bass Clarinet

From Concert Pitch to Written Bb Bass Clarinet Pitch

To transpose from concert pitch to written Bb bass clarinet pitch, raise the selected concert pitch by the interval of a Major 9th (a Major 2nd + an Octave).

Concert Pitch Transposition Written Bb Bass Clarinet Pitch
Bb ⬆ a Major 9th C
C ⬆ a Major 9th D
Eb ⬆ a Major 9th F
F ⬆ a Major 9th G

From Written Bb Bass Clarinet Pitch to Concert Pitch

To transpose from written Bb bass clarinet pitch to concert pitch, lower the selected written pitch by the interval of a Major 9th (a Major 2nd + an Octave).
Written Bb Bass Clarinet Pitch Transposition Concert Pitch
C ⬇ a Major 9th Bb
D ⬇ a Major 9th C
F ⬇ a Major 9th Eb
G ⬇ a Major 9th F
